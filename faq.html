FAQs

Where are you located?
We are based in Kingston, ON and serve all surrounding areas.
What are your service areas?
We offer moving services throughout Ontario and Quebec, including local and long-distance moves.
What are your rescheduling/cancellation policies?
You can reschedule or cancel up to 48 hours before your move with no fee.
Do you move long distance?
Yes, we handle both local and long-distance moves across provinces.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance for best availability.
What are your working hours?
Our team is available seven days a week, from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
Are your movers insured and background-checked?
Yes, all our movers are fully insured and have passed background checks.
What happens if something gets damaged?
We offer full insurance and will promptly resolve any damage claims.
How do I know my quote won't change last minute?
Our quotes are guaranteed with no hidden fees or surprise charges.
Do your movers pad and wrap all furniture items?
Absolutely—all furniture is padded and wrapped for maximum protection.
